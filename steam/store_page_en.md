# Steam Store Page Draft (EN)

## Title
Whispers of the Hollow Ward

## Short Description
Navigate a frozen asylum where AI-driven NPCs remember your choices, and every decision pushes you toward angelic mercy, demonic control, or eldritch corruption.

## Long Description
Whispers of the Hollow Ward is a narrative suspense-horror game focused on branching choices and AI-assisted character interaction.  
Across three chapters, you investigate a decaying ward where five major NPCs react to your behavior, your past decisions, and the current moral trend of the run.

### Key Features
- AI-driven NPC minds with role cards, goals, fears, and taboos
- 60 meaningful choices that shape trust, risk, and consequence
- 3 major routes: Angel & Kindness, Demon & Malice, Dark & Cthulhu
- 9 endings based on trend scores plus critical story flags
- Soft mature horror tone: occult tension, psychological dread, and forbidden text

## Suggested Tags
Psychological Horror, Choices Matter, Narrative, Mystery, Mature

## Content Warning
Contains psychological horror, violence implications, and mature thematic elements (non-explicit).
